ApexHR — Tenant Admin

How it works
1
Log in to your ApexHR account using your email and password.
2
Copy your access token from the response. Open F12 → Network tab → click the login request → look in the Response for accessToken.
3
Paste your ngrok base URL and token below in the Connection section, then click Save.
4
Fetch all tenants to see a list, or use the Danger Zone to wipe everything. You can also delete individual tenants from the list. These actions are permanent.
